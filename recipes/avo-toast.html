Avo On Toast

Delicious avo on toast is so healthy for you

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Avo
  • Balsanic vinegar
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Equiptment

  • Toaster
  • Sharp knife
  • fork

Steps

  1. Cut bread
  2. Put bread in toaster
  3. Cut avo
  4. Spread avo on toast with fork
  5. put balsanic vinegar on avo
  6. Put salt and pepper on avo
  7. Eat
